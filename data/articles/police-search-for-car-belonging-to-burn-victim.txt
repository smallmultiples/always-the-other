Police are looking for a car belonging to a woman who suffered extensive burns in a suspicious fire last week.The 29-year-old woman remains in a critical condition at Royal Perth Hospital after suffering burns to 60 per cent of her body.Police believe she was tied up and set on fire at a townhouse in Cannington.Detectives investigating the case say the woman's car, a 2008 orange hatchback, is missing from her address.It's licence plate is 1CVR 328.
